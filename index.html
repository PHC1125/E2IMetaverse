Center For Engineering Education Innovation in the Metaverse

This page served as a repositiory for the metaverse space used as a part of the teaching and learning innovation project headed by Professor Ben Chan in the Center for Engineering Education Innovation (E2I), School of Engineering in the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)

HKUST 2050

Students are asked to envision the future of HKUST in the year 2050.

World Culture

Students are asked to reimagine the culture of a specific region in a creative way.

Intercultural Peer Learning Program

A yearly exchange event between HKUST and SKKU in which students from both sides get to explore the unique culture of Hong Kong and Korea.

Collaboration with HK Tramways

In collaboration with HK Tramways, we created the "Be a Metaverse Creator" STEM Educatio module designed for secondary school students.

E2Island

A custom built environemnt for virtual metaverse-tracing and team building.
