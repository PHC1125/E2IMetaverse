Center For Engineering Education Innovation in the Metaverse

This page served as a repository for the metaverse space used as a part of the teaching and learning innovation project headed by Professor Ben Chan in the Center for Engineering Education Innovation (E2I) in the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)

HKUST 2050

Students are asked to envision the future of HKUST in the year 2050, exploring innovative possibilities and technological advancements.

World Culture

Students reimagine the culture of specific regions in creative ways, fostering cultural understanding and innovation.

Intercultural Peer Learning Program (IPLP)

A yearly exchange event between HKUST and SKKU, exploring the unique cultures of Hong Kong and Korea.

Collaboration with HK Tramways

"Be a Metaverse Creator" STEM Education module designed for secondary school students in partnership with HK Tramways.

E2Island

A custom-built environment for virtual metaverse-tracing and team building activities.
